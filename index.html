RoCK

RoCK stands for Robotics of Central Kitsap and is the robotics club for the Central Kitsap School District. We have members from three of the high schools such as Olympic, Klahowya, and Central. While we do have some members we are always looking for more so we hope you come join us after seeing some of what we do from this website.

What is Robotics

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots capable of performing tasks typically done by humans. These tasks can range from simple repetitive actions to complex decision-making. Robotics combines principles from engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, and electronics to create machines that can interact with their environment, process information, and carry out predefined operations. Robots can be used in a wide variety of industries, such as manufacturing, healthcare, and space exploration, where they help improve efficiency, safety, and accuracy. Advances in robotics continue to drive innovation, leading to more autonomous, intelligent, and adaptable machines.

What Do We Do

Here at RoCK we do a variety of things such as build robots, partake in events, join competitions, socialize, and a variety of other things. The robots we build are usually related to the competitions we join but we also make some for events at other schools. For the events we do they usually are related to showing the robots to other schools to hopefully show them how cool robotics is. The competitions we join are mostly hosted by FIRST robotics but we also will do a localized competition afterwards.
